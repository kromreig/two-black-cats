I grew up in rural Texas and spent a good portion of my adult life in Mexico City, where I worked in communications for a tech company. My education and professional background center around planning and understanding systems. Motivated by the desire to make the world better and hold powerful people to account, I am currently pursuing a dual graduate degree in computer science and journalism at Columbia University (expected 2019). My articles have been published in Gothamist,The Indypendent and Truthout.
